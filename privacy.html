Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Haris Delalić ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through our mobile application, Caffeine Curve.

1. No data collection

Caffeine Curve does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses.
  • We do not collect device identifiers (such as IDFA or IP addresses).
  • We do not collect or request access to your location data.

2. Local storage (UserDefaults)

The app stores user-defined preferences (such as caffeine targets or app settings) locally on your device using the UserDefaults system.

  • This data is stored strictly on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.
  • This data is used solely to maintain your settings across app launches.
  • You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app from your device.

3. No third-party services or AI

To ensure maximum privacy:

  • We do not use third-party SDKs (such as analytics, advertising, or crash reporting).
  • We do not utilize Artificial Intelligence (AI) models that process or "learn" from your data.
  • No data is shared with third-party providers.

4. Children's privacy

Because we do not collect any personal data, Caffeine Curve is safe for a general audience and does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

5. Your rights

As no data is collected or stored by us, there is no personal data for you to access, rectify, or delete from our systems. Your data remains entirely under your control on your local device.

6. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at caffeine-curve-privacy@delalic.com.
